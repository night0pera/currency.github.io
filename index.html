DOLLAR

A dollar is a unit of currency or money. The symbol for a dollar is $. A dollar bill is the equivalent of 100 cents There are also coins with the value of a dollar, although there are used less often in the US than in other countries

EURO

The euro is the offical currency of 19 of the 27 member states of the European Union. This group states is known as the eurozone or euro area and includes about 343 millions citizens as of 2019

RUPEE
